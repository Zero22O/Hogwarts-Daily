Godric Gryffindor

Among the legendary founders of Hogwarts, Godric Gryffindor stands as a symbol of bravery and unwavering honor. Born in the village that would one day be known as Godric's Hollow, he grew up with a fierce sense of justice and a noble heart that inspired those around Gryffindor believed that true greatness was defined not by bloodline or wealth, but by courage — the willingness to do what is right even when it is difficult. With this belief, he created a house that welcomed students who possessed daring spirits, bold hearts, and the strength to stand up for others. .
